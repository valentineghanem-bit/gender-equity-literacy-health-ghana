# Evidence — Methodology (spatial, ML & mediation)

- Brinda et al. 2015 — ecological GII→child-mortality design precedent (country level).
- Sen et al. 2023, *PLOS One* — SEM mediation with bootstrap: empowerment→ENC via skilled ANC (individual level).
- Abreha et al. 2020, *PLoS One* — MIMIC latent model: empowerment→child anaemia/stunting (dimension-specific).
- Spatial-analysis precedents in Ghana (low birth weight, malaria) — Moran's I / LISA at subnational scale.
- Shih et al. 2023, *JNCI* — ecological fallacy: area proxies can bias/flip coefficients.
- Fontanet et al. 2023, *Ann Surg Oncol*; Hogg et al. 2025, *PLOS One* — modifiable areal unit problem; rezoning sensitivity required.
- TRIPOD-AI / STROBE / RECORD-Spatial reporting standards applied.
