# Evidence — Introduction (burden & context)

- Cao et al. 2026, *PLOS One* — UN-IGME: sub-Saharan Africa under-five mortality ~69/1000 (2023); SDG 3.2 target 25/1000 by 2030; region not on track until ~mid-century.
- Bai-Sesay et al. 2025, *BMC Public Health* — up to 45% of under-five deaths in high-burden African countries attributable to economic inequality (WHO HEAT).
- Brinda et al. 2015, *BMC Public Health* — UNDP Gender Inequality Index positively associated with neonatal/infant/under-five mortality (138 countries; ecological).
- Kennedy et al. 2020, *Lancet Glob Health* — gender inequalities in health emerge in the first two decades of life (40 LMICs).
- Melesse et al. 2020, *BMJ Glob Health* — adolescent SRH stratified by education and wealth across 33 SSA countries.
- Wegmüller 2020 / Petry 2020 — Ghana anaemia north–south gradient and belt-specific drivers.
