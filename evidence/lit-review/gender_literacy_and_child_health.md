# Evidence — Literature review (gender, literacy & child health)

**Supporting**
- Yaya et al. 2018, *Reprod Health* — women's empowerment → contraceptive use (32 SSA, DHS).
- Ewerling et al. 2017, *Lancet Glob Health* — SWPER index (attitude-to-violence, social independence, decision-making); validates a multidimensional equity construct.
- Okyere et al. 2025, *Reprod Health* — education → service utilisation → child survival (indirect pathway).
- Madichie et al. 2026, *PLOS One* — secondary education ↓U5MR; literacy not independent of education in adjusted models (collinearity).
- Seidu et al. 2021, *J Public Health* — empowerment indicators → child-health-service uptake.

**Contrasting / qualifying**
- Chol Chol et al. 2019, *BMJ Open* — weak/paradoxical autonomy → maternal-care associations.
- Bliznashka et al. 2021, *PLoS Med* — no association for several child-development outcomes.
- Christian et al. 2023, *Matern Child Nutr* — effect moderated/reversed by household headship.
- Bessing et al. 2026, *BMJ Open* — same 2022 Ghana DHS: SWPER–ANC attenuated to non-significance after adjustment; northern disparities persist.
