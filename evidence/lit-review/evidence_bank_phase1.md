## [DISCUSSION / CONTRASTING] empowerment→health magnitude (≥2 per claim — satisfied, strengthened post-council)

## [METHODOLOGY / CONTRASTING] ecological-spatial design critique (council-required, now banked)
- **Shih et al. 2023, *JNCI* (24) — ecological fallacy: area proxies for individual SES can bias coefficients and FLIP their sign, leading to wrong-group targeting; misclassification underestimates effects (esp. rural).** https://consensus.app/papers/details/cc576cfe03ac5df39a6a71f4aeeaa9f3/
- **Fontanet et al. 2023, *Ann Surg Oncol* (8) — MAUP: results differ by areal unit chosen; rural areas show highly variable rates at low density.** https://consensus.app/papers/details/80665403adce50ba9fd5d1202879545c/
- **Hogg et al. 2025, *PLOS One* — MAUP simulation: aggregation/rezoning changes regression coefficients; sensitivity analysis to rezoning is required.** https://consensus.app/papers/details/f847caf7a7e75472b082f74b7a618418/
