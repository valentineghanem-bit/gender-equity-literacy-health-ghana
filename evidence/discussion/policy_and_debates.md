# Evidence — Discussion (outcomes, debates & policy)

**Outcomes context**
- Petry et al. 2020, *Matern Child Nutr* — anaemia determinants differ by climate belt (spatial heterogeneity).
- Yakubu & Salisu 2018; Ahinkorah et al. 2021 — adolescent fertility determinants (education, wealth, gender power), SSA DHS.
- Avelino et al. 2025, *Eur J Pediatr* — Mosley–Chen framework for U5 mortality determinants.
- Sule et al. 2022, *BMJ Glob Health* — six gendered MNCH dimensions.

**Debates (≥2 contrasting per major claim — satisfied)**
- Empowerment→health magnitude: Chol Chol 2019 (weak), Bliznashka 2021 (null), Christian 2023 (reversal), Bessing 2026 (attenuation on same data).
- Ecological/spatial design: Shih 2023 (ecological fallacy), Fontanet 2023 / Hogg 2025 (MAUP).

**Policy**
- Service-coverage ceiling (~89%) → upstream, spatially targeted girls'-education and gender-equity investment; SDG 3/4/5.
